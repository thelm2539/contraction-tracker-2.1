breathe & birth
Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 2026

Breathe & Birth is a contraction timing tool designed to help you and your birth partner track contractions during labour. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

What we collect

We collect only the data you actively enter into the app:

  • Contraction timing data — start time, duration, and the gap between contractions
  • Session codes — a randomly generated code used to sync data between two devices

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal identifying information.

How your data is stored

Contraction data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database, a service provided by Google LLC. Data is associated with your session code only — not with any account or identity. Anyone who knows your session code can view and add to the session data.

Firebase may collect technical usage data such as IP addresses and device information as part of operating the service. This is governed by Google's Firebase Privacy Policy.

How your data is used

Your contraction data is used solely to display timing information within the app. It is never:

  • Sold to third parties
  • Used for advertising or marketing
  • Used for data mining or analytics
  • Shared with any party other than the Firebase infrastructure required to sync between devices

Data retention & deletion

Session data remains in Firebase until you delete it. You can delete all data for a session at any time using the Clear All button within the app. This permanently removes all contraction records from our database.

If you do not use a session for an extended period, data may be automatically removed as part of routine database maintenance.

Medical disclaimer

Breathe & Birth is a timing tool only. It does not diagnose labour, medical conditions, or complications. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your midwife, obstetrician, or care provider when making decisions about your labour and birth.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your rights

Because we do not collect personally identifying information, we cannot identify individual users. However, you can remove all data you have entered at any time using the Clear All feature in the app.

If you have questions about your data, please contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
